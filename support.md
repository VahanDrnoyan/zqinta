# zQinta — Support

We're a small indie team, and we're glad to help.

> **App:** zQinta · **Platform:** macOS 15+ · **Download:** [zqinta.com](https://www.zqinta.com)

---

## Contact us (fastest)

📧 **Email:** [support@zqinta.com](mailto:support@zqinta.com)

We aim to reply within **2 business days**.

---

## Report a bug or suggest a feature

Two ways:

1. **Email us** (above) — best if you're not a developer.
2. **Open a GitHub issue:** [VahanDrnoyan/CascadeAI/issues](https://github.com/VahanDrnoyan/CascadeAI/issues)

When you open an issue, a short form will prompt you for the details we need (macOS version, app version, steps to reproduce).

---

## Before you reach out

It helps if you include:

- **macOS version** ( → About This Mac)
- **App version** (zQinta → Settings → About)
- **What you tried**, and what you expected vs. what happened
- A **screenshot or screen recording** if something looks wrong

Also check that you're on the **latest version** — zQinta auto-updates via Sparkle. You can also re-download the latest build from [zqinta.com](https://www.zqinta.com).

---

## Subscriptions, billing & refunds

Billing is handled directly by **Stripe** (the app is distributed outside the Mac App Store):

- **Manage or cancel:** open zQinta → Subscription tab → Manage Subscription (opens the Stripe customer portal).
- **Refunds:** email [support@zqinta.com](mailto:support@zqinta.com) and we'll sort it out.
- Tiers & features: see [zqinta.com#pricing](https://www.zqinta.com#pricing).

Your data is **never deleted** if a subscription lapses — you drop to the Free tier and everything stays accessible.

---

## Your data & privacy

- Knowledge maps are **stored locally on your Mac**.
- **Premium** can sync across your Macs via iCloud (CloudKit Private Database).
- If you bring your own AI key (OpenAI / Anthropic / Seedance), it's stored in the macOS **Keychain** — we never see it or your queries.
- No trackers, no ads, no data selling.

Full details: **[Privacy Policy](privacy.html)**.
